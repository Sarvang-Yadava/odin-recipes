Lasagna

Description

Lasagna, also known as lasagne in plural form, is a type of pasta made in very wide , flat sheets. It consists of stacked layers of pasta alternating with fillings such as ragu(ground meats and tomato sauce), vegetables etc. This may also be topped with grated cheese which beautifully gets melted when baked and looks like a golden sheet on top.

Ingredients needed

  • Extra-Virgin Olive Oil
  • Cremini Mushrooms
  • Red Onion
  • Red Bell Pepper
  • Baby Spinach
  • Low-Moisture Mozarella Cheese
  • Egg
  • Marinara Sauce
  • Lasagna Noodles (Most Important Ofc)
  • Basil Leaves and Salt

Directions

  1. Prep and Vegetables

    Preheat the oven to 350 F and lightly coat a baking dish with cooking spray. Saute the mushrooms, onion, bell pepper in 2 tbsp oil over medium-high heat for few minutes until tender. Add tomatoes. salt and crushed pepper and cook for 1 more minute.

  2. More Prep

    Heat 1 tbsp of oil over medium heat and add spinach then cook until wilted. After cooling, squeeze out excess liquid then mix it in cooked vegetables.

  3. Ricotta Mixture

    Combine 1/2 cup of mozarella, ricotta and Parmesan.Then egg and 1/4 teaspoon salt in meduim oil. Stir until well blended.

  4. Avengers Assemble!

    In Layer 1, spread 1 cup of marinara in the baking dish then add 4 noodles, 1 cup of ricotta mixture, and 2 cups of vegetable mixture. Repeat this layer one more time. For final layer, top with 4 noodles and remaining 1 cup of ricotta mixture, and 1 cup of marinara. Sprinkle with mozzarella and Parmesan for golden crown. Cover whole with a foil.

  5. Bake and Serve

    Bake it for about 30 minutes. Remove the foil and bake uncovered for another 20 minutes until top is golden brown. Cool and serve!
